# Voice and Tone

## Voice

RidgeLink's voice is professional, direct, technically credible, practical, and calm under pressure.

## Tone

Use a confident but not arrogant tone.

Prefer:

> RidgeLink designs secure, maintainable infrastructure.

Avoid:

> RidgeLink revolutionizes world-class digital transformation outcomes.
